About Jog Falls

Jog Falls, located in Karnataka, India, is the second-highest plunge waterfall in the country. It is formed by the Sharavathi River and offers a breathtaking view of cascading water amidst lush greenery.

Surrounded by dense forests, Jog Falls is a haven for nature lovers and photographers.

With a height of 830 feet, Jog Falls is one of the tallest plunge waterfalls in India.

The area offers trekking opportunities for adventure enthusiasts.

Plan Your Visit

Best Time to Visit: August to December

Location: Sagara, Shivamogga District, Karnataka, India

How to Reach: Easily accessible by road, rail, and air via Shivamogga and nearby cities.

Travel Tips

  • Visit during the monsoon season (June to September) for the best views.
  • Wear comfortable shoes if planning to explore hiking trails.
  • Carry rain gear and water bottles when visiting during the rainy season.
  • Arrive early to avoid crowds and enjoy the serene atmosphere.
